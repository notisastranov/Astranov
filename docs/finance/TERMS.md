# Astranov Terms of Service - Financial Clauses

## 1. Marketplace Intermediation (Model 1)
Astranov acts as a digital intermediary connecting Customers with independent Service Providers. Astranov is not the provider of the core services and assumes no liability for the quality or execution of such services.

## 2. Brokerage Services (Model 2)
In specific transactions (e.g., Device Sales, Real Estate), Astranov acts as a Broker. The Brokerage Fee is earned upon successful completion of the transaction as defined in the specific Brokerage Contract.

## 3. Platform Usage Fees (Model 3)
Users agree to pay a non-refundable Platform Usage Fee for specific digital actions (e.g., publishing a post). This fee is a direct charge for the use of Astranov's digital infrastructure.

## 4. Self-Billing Authorization
By activating a Provider Profile, the Provider explicitly authorizes Astranov to issue invoices on their behalf to Customers ("Self-Billing"). The Provider remains responsible for the accuracy of their tax data and the timely declaration of such income to the Greek Tax Authorities.

## 5. Wallet & Funds Handling
Funds received by Astranov from Customers on behalf of Providers are held in a dedicated "Liability Account" and do not constitute revenue for Astranov until the commission is extracted. Payouts are subject to the Settlement Schedule (Monthly).

## 6. Refunds & Disputes
Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of service completion. Astranov reserves the right to withhold funds in the event of a dispute or suspected fraud. Chargebacks initiated via payment processors will result in an immediate debit from the Provider's balance.

## 7. Tax Compliance (myDATA)
All transactions are reported to the Greek Independent Authority for Public Revenue (IAPR) via the myDATA framework. Users are responsible for providing valid Tax Identification Numbers (AFM).
