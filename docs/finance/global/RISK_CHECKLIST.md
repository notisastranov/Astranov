# Global Expansion Risk & Compliance Checklist

## 1. Tax & Accounting
- [ ] **VAT/GST Registration**: Is the platform registered in the target country?
- [ ] **Threshold Tracking**: Are we monitoring GMV for local tax nexus?
- [ ] **Chart of Accounts**: Does the local entity have a compliant COA?
- [ ] **FX Risk**: Are we hedging currency exposure for large settlements?

## 2. Legal & Regulatory
- [ ] **Terms of Service**: Has the local TOS been reviewed by local counsel?
- [ ] **Data Privacy**: Does the system comply with local data residency laws?
- [ ] **Payment License**: Do we need an EMI or PSP license to hold funds in this country?
- [ ] **Consumer Rights**: Does the refund policy match local mandatory laws?

## 3. Operational
- [ ] **Banking**: Is a local bank account required for settlements?
- [ ] **Support**: Can we handle disputes in the local language?
- [ ] **Audit**: Is the system ready for a local tax audit?
- [ ] **Reporting**: Is the myDATA/SAF-T/MTD integration verified?

## 4. Security
- [ ] **KYC/KYB**: Are we verifying providers according to local AML laws?
- [ ] **Fraud**: Are country-specific fraud patterns being monitored?
- [ ] **Encryption**: Does the data encryption meet local standards?
